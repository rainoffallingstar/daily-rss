History of the Self: Aging

Defeating old age? In 1899, Elie Metchnikoff woke up in Paris to learn he had done just that. At least, that’s what the newspaper headlines said. Before long he was inundated with mail from people begging him to help them live forever. The only problem? He didn’t know how to do it. At the time, Metchnikoff was one of the world’s most famous scientists. And he believed aging was a disease he could cure.
