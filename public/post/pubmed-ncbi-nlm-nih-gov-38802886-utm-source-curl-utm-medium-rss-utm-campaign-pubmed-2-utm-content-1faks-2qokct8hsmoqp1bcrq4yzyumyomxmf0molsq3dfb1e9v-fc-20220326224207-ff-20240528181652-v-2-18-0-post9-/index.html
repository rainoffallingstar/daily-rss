CONCLUSIONS: The survey revealed a set of 169 data elements recommended for health registries. When developing a registry, this set could be valuable help in selecting the metadata appropriate to answer the registry’s research questions. However, due to the high specificity of research questions, data elements beyond this set will be needed to cover the whole range of interests of a register.
