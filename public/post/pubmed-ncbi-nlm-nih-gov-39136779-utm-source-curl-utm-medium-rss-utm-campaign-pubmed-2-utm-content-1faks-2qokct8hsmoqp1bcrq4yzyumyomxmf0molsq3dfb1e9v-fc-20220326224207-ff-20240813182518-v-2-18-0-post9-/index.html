Biological effects of a zinc-substituted borosilicate bioactive glass on human bone marrow derived stromal cells <em>in vitro</em> and in a critical-size femoral defect model in rats <em>in vivo</em>

This study investigated the addition of zinc (Zn) to 0106-B1-BG as an approach to improve this BG’s biological properties.
