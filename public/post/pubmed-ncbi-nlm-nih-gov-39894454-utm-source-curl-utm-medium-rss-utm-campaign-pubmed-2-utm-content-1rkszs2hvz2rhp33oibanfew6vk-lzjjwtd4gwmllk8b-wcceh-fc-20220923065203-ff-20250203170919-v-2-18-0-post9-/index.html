Insights into treatment of patients with mycosis fungoides or Sézary syndrome using mogamulizumab

CONCLUSIONS: MAVORIC demonstrated greater efficacy with mogamulizumab vs. vorinostat in relapsed/refractory patients with CTCL, including those with more advanced disease. Concomitant steroid use improved ORR and PFS but did not impact vorinostat outcomes. Overall responses occurred more frequently in mogamulizumab-treated patients that developed lymphopenia than those that did not.
